What is Cursive Data

Cursive Data is a web service for turning real time data into evolving data visualisations. The visualisations can be embedded into a website, or they can be sent to an internet connected plotter that will draw the data as it arrives.

Where did the idea come from?

Matt Venn was wondering how to make energy data more accessible for those not interested in graphs or tables of data. The aim became to create physical representation of the energy data. Among many ideas considered, a v plotter type robot was chosen for simplicity and cost. A document further explaining the idea is available here.
